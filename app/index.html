YT-Converter

I created the yt-converter to simplify a process i both; found myself doing alot, and finding doing the task as tedious.

I frequently converted songs from Youtube to MP3 to add to my spotify playlists manually, which kind of became a tiresome task, especially so for a large amount of songs. This project is designed to make said process much easier and faster, by automating it with just a few clicks.

The converter allows you to paste a Youtube playlist or song link, convert it to MP3, then easily add it to spotify using their local files setting.

Guide

1. Get the Youtube Link - Go to Youtube and find the playlist / song you want to convert.
- Copy the URL from your browser's address bar. For example, for a playlist, it would look like: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=YOUR_PLAYLIST_ID

2. Paste the link in the YT-Converter - In the input field below, paste the Youtube link you just copied.
- Hit the "Convert" button.

3. Add the MP3 Files to Spotify (if you are adding) - Open Spotify and go to Edit > Preferences (or Spotify > Settings on macOS.)
- Scroll down to the Local Files section, and toggle on the Show Local Files option.
- Click Add a Source and browse for the folder where your MP3 files are saved. (e.g, C:/Users/JohnDoe/Path/To/Folder )
- Spotify will now display the songs in the Local Files section, and you can add them to any of your playlists.

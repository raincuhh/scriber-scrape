yt-spotify-mp3-converter

I created the yt-spotify-converter to simplify a process i both; found myself doing alot, and finding doing the task as tedious.

I frequently converted songs from Youtube to MP3 (to add to my spotify playlists manually), which kind of became a tiresome task, especially so for a large amount of songs. This app is designed to make said process much easier and faster, by automating it with just a few clicks.

The converter allows you to paste a Youtube / Spotify playlist or song link, convert it to MP3, to the do whatever you want with.

Faq

Getting private playlist links?

Click the link spotify/youtube button.

Follow the steps to link your spotify/youtube account. (we only access reading your private / public playlists.) (

Select the spotify/youtube playlist from the selection down below. (after linking spotify, it will list out your private playlists for you to choose.)

Select a folder to save your playlist mp3 songs to, and click the "Convert" button.

Adding the MP3 files to Spotify?

Open Spotify and go to Edit > Preferences, or Spotify > Settings on macOS. Scroll down to the Local Files, and toggle on the Show Local Files option.

- Click Add a Source and browse for the folder where your MP3 files are saved. (e.g, C:/Users/JohnDoe/Path/To/Folder/Chosen )

Spotify will now display the songs in the Local Files section, and you can add them to any of your playlists.
